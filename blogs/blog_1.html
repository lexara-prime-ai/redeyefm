Grimes' fifth album, Miss Anthropocene, is a captivating piece of art that delves deep into themes of climate change, environmental destruction, and the human condition.

From start to finish, this album transports listeners to a dystopian future where humanity's fate hangs in the balance. The album opener, "So Heavy I Fell Through the Earth," immediately draws listeners in with its haunting melody and Grimes' ethereal vocals. Highlights include "Violence" and "My Name is Dark," with their catchy hooks and unique production techniques.

One of the album's standout songs is "Delete Forever," which powerfully tackles the devastating consequences of the opioid crisis.
