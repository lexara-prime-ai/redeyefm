Self Improvement

The recent rise in self-improvement culture has sparked a drive among many to optimize their lives and reach perfection.

To begin with, the continuous strain to better oneself can lead to obsessive self-criticism and doubt. People may feel they are not making enough progress in spite of their hard work, which can bring about feelings of despair, dejection, and exhaustion. This pressure may also cause comparison and competition between others that lead to a feeling of inadequacy.
